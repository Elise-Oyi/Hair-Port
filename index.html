About Beauty & Hair Wave

Hair Port is the best in the beauty and cosmetology industry in Ghana.Geared to fashion-savvy clients, we are experienced growing salon in Ghana. established in 2020,grown in expertise and experience providing you with professional hair care, nail care, foot care and specialized body care solutions.
